About Me

Hi, I’m Aluko Jesutofunmi, a passionate Frontend Developer dedicated to crafting engaging, responsive, and accessible web experiences. I specialize in turning creative ideas into interactive interfaces using modern tools like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and Tailwind CSS. With a strong eye for design and a focus on user experience, I bring concepts to life through clean code, smooth animations, and mobile-first development. Whether it's building fast-loading websites or integrating APIs, I enjoy every step of the process—from wireframes to deployment. I’m constantly learning and staying updated with the latest trends in web development and UI/UX. My goal is to build digital products that not only look great but also solve real-world problems. When I’m not coding, I enjoy exploring design systems, contributing to open source, and helping others grow in tech.
